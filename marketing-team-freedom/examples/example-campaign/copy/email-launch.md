Canvas is infinite now.

Before today, every canvas had a soft edge — you'd hit a limit, start a new one, and lose the spatial thread you were building. That's gone. Open any canvas, zoom out, and keep going. Last month's notes are still there, at the edge of this month's.

Nothing else changed. Same capture, same stickers, same sync. The ceiling just left.

If you've been splitting projects across canvases for navigability — you can merge them now. We added a one-tap zoom-to-fit so the whole thing is always one gesture away, even if there are ten thousand elements.

Tested on iPads from 2019 onward at 10,000+ elements. It holds.
