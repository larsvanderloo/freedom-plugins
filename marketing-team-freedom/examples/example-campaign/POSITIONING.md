# Positioning — Fictional Product "Parallax Notes"

## One-liner

A note-taking app for people who think in diagrams, not paragraphs.

## Category

Visual-first note-taking. Adjacent to Notion, Obsidian, Apple Notes — but distinct from all three. Closest neighbour is Heptabase.

## Key differentiator

The only note app where every note is born as a canvas; text is the fallback, not the default. Every other app is the reverse.

## Proof points

- Default view for a new note is a blank canvas with drawing/sticker/shape tools. Text is one of many elements.
- Import a PDF or image and it appears on the canvas with markup tools around it — not inside a text wrapper.
- Spatial relationships between notes are first-class: proximity, grouping, nesting by location, not by folder hierarchy.
- Beta users (n=47) reported 3× higher retention at week 4 than a baseline text-first tool (internal study, March 2026).

## Why now?

- Tablet/iPad note-taking has matured to where pen-first workflows are common — audience exists
- LLMs have eaten linear-text summarisation; value has moved to visual-thinking workflows they can't yet replicate
- Heptabase has legitimised "canvas-first" as a category descriptor in the market
