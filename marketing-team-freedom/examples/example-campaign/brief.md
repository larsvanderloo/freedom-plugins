# Brief — Launch email for "Infinite Canvas" feature

**Date:** 2026-04-25
**Author:** Example / demo brief
**Deadline:** 2026-05-02 — ships same day as the feature is enabled for all users
**Status:** approved

---

## Goal

**Primary objective:** Drive existing users to open Parallax and try the infinite-canvas feature within 48 hours of the email hitting their inbox.

**Success metric:** 35% of recipients who've logged in in the last 30 days open the app within 48 hours of email send. Directional: share rate on social tags for the feature.

**Funnel stage:** Retention / feature-adoption (existing users).

---

## Audience

**Primary persona (from AUDIENCE.md):** Maya, the visual-thinker designer.

**Specific cut for this piece:** Active users who've created ≥3 canvases in the last 30 days. (Not the cold newsletter list — they get a different piece next week.)

**Awareness stage:** Most-aware. They use the product already. They know our voice.

**What they already know:** What Parallax is. How canvases work. The existing per-canvas bounds.

---

## Message

**Messaging pillar:** "The canvas is the product" — the pillar that says every upgrade to canvas primitives is a first-class event.

**One idea this piece delivers:** Canvas is infinite now. Zoom out and last month's notes are still there, at the edge of this month's.

**Proof points:**
- Feature shipped, enabled today for all users
- One-tap zoom-to-fit to see the whole thing
- Performance on 2019+ iPads verified at 10,000 elements

**What NOT to say:**
- Don't compare to competitors by name
- Don't hint at future features (keep focused)
- Don't over-sell — this is an upgrade, not a re-platform

---

## Call to action

**Primary CTA (exact words):** "Zoom out in Parallax"
**Where it leads:** Deep-link into the user's most recently opened canvas.
**Secondary CTA:** "Watch the 30-second demo" → link to a 30s silent loop.

---

## Channel & format

**Channel:** Email
**Format:** Plain-HTML email (no headers, no template chrome). Matches our existing newsletter style.
**Length target:** 120-180 words in the body. Subject line: 40-50 chars.
**Tone note:** This is an active-user email. Speak as teammate. Don't re-explain what Parallax is.

---

## Constraints

- Subject + preview text must render on iPhone mobile (primary read device for this persona, ~70%)
- No emoji in subject line (BRAND-VOICE.md rule)
- Must link the 30s demo — it ships the same day
- Legal — no pricing mention in this email (pricing changes next month, separate comms)
