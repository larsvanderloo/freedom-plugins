# Audience — Parallax Notes

## Primary persona: "Maya, the visual-thinker designer"

- **Job-to-be-done (functional):** Capture ideas mid-flow without being forced into paragraphs or bullets before she's ready. Make connections visible spatially.
- **Emotional JTBD:** Feel like her tool is on her side, not fighting her. Avoid the shame of "I have 400 notes and can't find anything".
- **Social JTBD:** Be seen as the teammate who shows up with the clearest artifact in a brainstorm. Her canvases become the meeting.
- **Current solution:** Apple Notes + Freeform + Figma for serious sessions. Switches context 4-6 times a day.
- **Why current solution is broken:** Freeform doesn't sync ideas back to her long-term memory. Apple Notes can't do spatial. Figma is overkill and slow for quick capture.
- **Awareness stage:** Solution-aware (knows canvas-first tools exist). Has tried Heptabase. Didn't stick because of sync reliability issues.
- **Where they hang out:** Dribbble comments, r/ProductManagement, r/ObsidianMD, Twitter/X design community, a few Discord servers.
- **Words they use (not yours):**
  - "Canvas", "board" (not "workspace")
  - "Capture", "jot", "throw it down" (not "record", "document")
  - "Shape", "sticky", "card" (not "block", "element", "widget")
  - "Make it make sense" (self-talk about messy notes)
- **Words that turn them off:**
  - "Knowledge base", "PKM", "second brain" (too earnest / too Obsidian-coded)
  - "Productivity suite" / "all-in-one"
  - "AI-powered" (she's skeptical when it's the lead claim)
  - Any "unlock", "supercharge", "level up"

## Secondary persona: "Sam, the academic researcher"

- **JTBD:** Connect readings, diagrams, and handwritten notes into a thinking canvas for ongoing projects. Reference-heavy.
- **Why secondary:** Similar visual-thinking need but different workflow (longer timelines, more citation pressure). Product serves them but the primary copy should not lead with academic framing — it alienates Maya.
- **Key vocabulary to respect when writing to Sam specifically:** "source", "thread", "claim", "reference".
- **Channels where Sam concentrates:** academic Twitter, Zettelkasten forum, specific subreddits.
